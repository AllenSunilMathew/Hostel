About Amal's Gents Hostel

Providing a safe, comfortable home away from home since 2023

Our Evening View

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Mr. Amal Kumar, our hostel began as a small 10-room facility with a vision to provide affordable yet quality accommodation for students and working professionals.

What started as a modest venture has now grown into one of the most trusted hostel names in the region. Over the years, we've expanded our facilities, improved our services, and most importantly, built a community that feels like family.

Today, we house over 200 residents with modern amenities while maintaining the personal touch that made us successful in the first place.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape everything we do

Our Mission

To provide safe, affordable, and comfortable accommodation that fosters community, personal growth, and academic/professional success for all our residents.

We commit to maintaining clean facilities, offering nutritious food options, and creating an environment conducive to both study and relaxation.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier hostel that transforms temporary stays into lifelong relationships and memories, while setting the standard for quality student housing.

We aim to expand our reach while maintaining our core values of integrity, community, and excellence in service.

Our Journey

Milestones in our growth and development

2023 Jan - Humble Beginnings

Amal's Gents Hostel opens with just 10 rooms in a small building, serving local college students.

2023 Mar - First Expansion

We acquire the adjacent property, doubling our capacity to 20 rooms and adding a common study area.

2023 Dec - Dining Services

We launch our in-house mess service providing nutritious, homestyle meals to residents.

2023 Dec - Current Facility

Opened our new wing with premium rooms, and a rooftop View.
